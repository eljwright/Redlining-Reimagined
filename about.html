About This Project

Redlining Reimagined is an educational website designed for high school and university students in the Seattle area. It was created as my final year capstone project for my BA in Digital Cultures and Media at Newcastle University. My aim was to create an engaging way for students to learn about the history and legacy of redlining in their own city. Through the two primary components, the story map and the comparison map, it aims to deepen understanding of systemic racial inequalities and encourage critical reflection on their lasting impact in Seattle.
